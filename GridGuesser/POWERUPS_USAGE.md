# Power-Ups Usage Guide 🎯

## Quick Start

### Using Power-Ups

**Power-ups can ONLY be used on YOUR turn!**

When it's your turn:
1. The power-up buttons will be **fully colored** (purple gradient)
2. Click a power-up to activate it
3. Follow the on-screen instructions

When it's NOT your turn:
- Power-up buttons are **grayed out**
- A message shows: "Power-ups available on your turn"
- Wait for your turn to use them!

## Power-Up Details

### Skip Turn (5 Points)
**How to Use:**
1. Click "Skip Turn" button
2. ✅ Immediately activated!
3. Opponent loses their next turn

**Visual Feedback:**
- Notification appears: "Player X used Skip Turn!"
- Turn indicator stays on you

---

### Reveal 2x2 (8 Points) - INTERACTIVE MODE
**How to Use:**
1. Click "Reveal 2x2" button
2. Purple instruction box appears
3. **Hover over opponent's grid** - you'll see a **purple 2x2 square** highlighting 4 tiles
4. Move your mouse to position the square where you want
5. Click anywhere in the grid to reveal those 4 tiles!

**Visual Features:**
- **Crosshair cursor** when hovering over opponent's grid
- **Purple ring** highlights the 2x2 area (4 tiles)
- **Purple glow** shows exactly which tiles will be revealed
- Area adjusts near edges (won't go off the grid)

**Tips:**
- Position the top-left corner carefully
- Near edges, only available tiles are highlighted
- Click "Cancel" in the instruction box to exit without using

---

### Nuke (15 Points)
**How to Use:**
1. Click "Nuke" button
2. ✅ Immediately activated!
3. ALL 100 tiles revealed instantly

**Visual Feedback:**
- Notification: "Player X nuked the image!"
- Entire opponent grid becomes visible
- Perfect for when you need to see the full picture!

---

## Visual Indicators

### When You CAN Use a Power-Up:
✅ Purple/pink gradient background  
✅ Solid border  
✅ Hover effect (scales up)  
✅ Bright purple cost badge  
✅ "Your Turn" status active  

### When You CANNOT Use a Power-Up:
❌ Gray background  
❌ Faded appearance  
❌ "Not your turn" or "Not enough points" message  
❌ Disabled cursor  
❌ No hover effects  

### 2x2 Mode Active:
🎯 Purple instruction box at top of sidebar  
🎯 Crosshair cursor over opponent's grid  
🎯 Purple ring highlights 4 tiles  
🎯 Hover preview updates in real-time  

## Strategy Tips

1. **Save Points Wisely**: Don't rush to use power-ups immediately
2. **Use Skip Turn**: Great when opponent is close to guessing
3. **2x2 on Corners**: Target areas that look interesting
4. **Nuke Strategically**: Best used when you're confident you can guess
5. **Turn Timing**: Plan power-up usage for your turn

## Keyboard & Mouse

- **Mouse Hover**: Shows 2x2 preview (in reveal2x2 mode)
- **Left Click**: Confirms power-up action
- **Move Mouse**: Repositions 2x2 area in real-time

## Common Questions

**Q: Why can't I use power-ups?**  
A: Check if it's your turn AND you have enough points

**Q: Can I cancel Reveal 2x2 after activating?**  
A: Yes! Click "Cancel" in the purple instruction box

**Q: Does Reveal 2x2 reveal already-revealed tiles?**  
A: No, it only reveals new tiles in the 2x2 area

**Q: What happens if I use Skip Turn on my turn?**  
A: You keep your turn, and opponent loses their next turn

**Q: Can I use multiple power-ups in one turn?**  
A: Not simultaneously, but you can use one after another if you have points
